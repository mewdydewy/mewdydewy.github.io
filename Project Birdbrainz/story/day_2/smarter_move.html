"Nothing's adding up here," Ari laments, after you get a safe distance away. "I don't know what we just saw, but I swear it was Ray's mom, or at least looks like her”. They sigh and turn around to you and Zimmer.

"Well?" they ask.

From behind you, Zimmer says "Something's been bothering me - when was the last time you heard from Ray? What was the last thing he said to you?"

"Saturday afternoon," Ari replies, "he said he had to cancel on us because… because his dog was sick!"

A horrible look of foreboding passes across Zimmer’s face.

"We have to go to his place, the long way".

With that, you all set off on the wide trail towards the street.
