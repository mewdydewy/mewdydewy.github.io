The snow is still falling hard, and your knees and shins have grown tired from spending all afternoon traversing it. By this point, only a small amount of natural light sustains what little visibility is left.

You have arrived at Ray Augur's house, and you start to clamber up the terrace in front of his home.

Rising high above the hills of snow and ice in Ray’s backyard is a dense forest of enormous, crystalline spires contorting upwards in looping spirals, like a thicket of white, shoelacing bamboo trees.

"No, that can't be…" he trails off and looks at you.

"Ghost pipes."

You steel your nerves in preparation of whatever the source of this vile garden is.
